FAQs

  1. Does Mt Rushmore Education provide test preparation for the TOEFL and/or GRE?
    2. No. But we do have connections with a number of test preparation companies that specialize in TOEFL/GRE training. We worked closely with many of them, and can provide recommendations if you prefer. We help you choose the appropriate tests and decide when to take them.
  2. Do you only work with high GPA students?
    3. No. GPA cannot justify how talented you are. So long as you have the desire, we will help you discover your own educational pathway that fits you.
  3. How late is too late to start?
    4. If you can, start as early as possible. But it's never been too late to start working with us.
  4. How much is the service?
    5. It depends. We have multiple solutions available. It's better to schedule a free consultation first, then we will find out a solution which fits your need.
